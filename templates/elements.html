Who we are:

Here at responder, we are a group of four high school students who have created this service to help seniors around the world. We firmly believe that getting to a hospital in times of need is a right and not a privilage. Thus, Responder helps the generations before us get access to healthcare as soon as possible, and we are proud to be leaders in our community!
